About Me

My name is Cheng-Jun Wang (aka 王成军/Frank), a Ph.D candidate in the Department of Media & Communication, City University of Hong Kong. Currently, I am also a research member of Web Mining Lab.

I received my master degree from Beijing University, and I got my bachelor degree from Lanzhou University. You can find my CV here.

Research Interest

Primarily, I study how information diffuses online, e.g., my dissertation focuses on information diffusion on social media (e.g. Digg, YouTube, Weibo & Twitter). Additionally, I am interested in statistics, network science, and agent-based models. I document my thoughts on my blog (English, Chinese) and the Github.
